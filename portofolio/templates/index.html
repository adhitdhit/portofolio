During the IoT Aquaculture research project at BRIN Bandung, I gained hands-on experience in applying Internet of Things (IoT) technologies to the aquaculture sector. Power Supply Amplifier

A Power Supply Amplifier is a crucial component in audio systems, responsible for boosting the electrical signal and delivering it to the speakers with enough power to produce sound. It converts low-voltage signals from a source device into higher voltage signals suitable for driving the speakers. The amplifier ensures stable power delivery while maintaining sound quality and reducing distortion. It is commonly used in various applications, such as home theater systems, public address systems, and musical instruments, providing the necessary power to drive speakers effectively.
